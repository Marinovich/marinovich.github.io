DEATH MAZE

Discription

Are you ready to plunge into the world of new adventures and unforgettable trips? Welcome to the Death Maze! Be careful! By pressing the start button, you are infected with an unknown virus that will kill you if you do not find the medicine at the exit from the maze. But your new friends are waiting for you. Be careful, because when you approach them, the effect of the virus increases and you have less and less time left. By the way, you all the same can not get out of it! Good Luck!
